NY PREMIERE
OPERA

Crossing

Oct 3—Oct 8

Matthew Aucoin, the prodigious young composer dubbed contemporary opera’s “rising wunderkind” (The New York Times), partners with Tony Award-winning director Diane Paulus (Hair, Pippin) on a timely new portrait of Walt Whitman and a nation in disarray. Set in a DC sick ward during Whitman’s years as a Civil War nurse, Crossing features baritone Rod Gilfry and Boston-based chamber orchestra A Far Cry, under the baton of Aucoin himself.
